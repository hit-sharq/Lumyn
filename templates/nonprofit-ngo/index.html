Our Mission

Why we exist

Hope Rising Foundation was founded in 2012 with a simple belief: that sustainable change comes from within communities. We don't impose solutions — we partner with local leaders, educators, and entrepreneurs to build lasting impact.

From rural water projects in Western Kenya to vocational training centres in Nairobi's informal settlements, every initiative is community-led and outcome-driven.

What We Do

Our programs

Clean Water

Boreholes, rainwater harvesting, and water filtration systems for rural communities lacking safe drinking water.

Education

Scholarships, school construction, teacher training, and digital literacy programs for children and youth.

Economic Empowerment

Vocational training, microfinance, and business incubation for women and youth entrepreneurs.

Healthcare

Mobile clinics, maternal health programmes, and mental health support in underserved areas.
