About Us

Our mission

Grace Community Church is a multi-generational, multicultural church in the heart of Nairobi. We believe in creating a space where everyone can encounter God, find community, and discover their purpose.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and serve our city with excellence and integrity. Whether you're a lifelong believer or simply curious, you have a place here.
